Automated Cassava Weeder Robot

Designed a 4-wheel autonomous cassava weeding robot, developing the complete mechanical system from concept to fabrication-ready CAD. The project includes a differential drive chassis, rotary blade weeding mechanism, protective shroud, and modular enclosures, supported by detailed 2D technical drawings and a motion animation.

Photo-Electrocoagulation Water Treatment Unit

Designed a compact Photo-Electrocoagulation (Photo-EC) system for treating petroleum-contaminated water. The project integrates aluminum electrodes and UV-C treatment in a cost-effective acrylic reactor, with provisions for foam removal, floc filtration, and safe electrical operation. The final design includes a complete CAD assembly, technical drawings, and an operational animation ready for fabrication and testing.

Open Channel Flow Teaching Apparatus With Fabricated Supply Tank

This project involved the design of an open channel flow laboratory apparatus through reverse engineering of an existing system. A fabricated supply tank was recreated as a fully parametric SolidWorks model using sheet metal features and assigned AISI 304 stainless steel. The assembly was validated through static simulation under hydrostatic pressure, with detailed 2D drawings, renders, and reports produced for fabrication and documentation.
