PROJECT 03

Open Channel Flow Teaching Apparatus With Fabricated Supply Tank

This project involved the design of an open channel flow laboratory apparatus through reverse engineering of an existing system. A fabricated supply tank was recreated as a fully parametric SolidWorks model using sheet metal features and assigned AISI 304 stainless steel. The assembly was validated through static simulation under hydrostatic pressure, with detailed 2D drawings, renders, and reports produced for fabrication and documentation.
