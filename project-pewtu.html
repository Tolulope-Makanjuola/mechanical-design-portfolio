Photo-Electrocoagulation Water Treatment Unit

Designed a compact Photo-Electrocoagulation (Photo-EC) system for treating petroleum-contaminated water. The project integrates aluminium electrodes and UV-C treatment in a cost-effective acrylic reactor, with provisions for foam removal, floc filtration, and safe electrical operation. The final design includes a complete CAD assembly, technical drawings, and an operational animation ready for fabrication and testing.
