Automated Cassava Weeder Robot

Designed a 4-wheel autonomous cassava weeding robot, developing the complete mechanical system from concept to fabrication-ready CAD. The project includes a differential drive chassis, rotary blade weeding mechanism, protective shroud, and modular enclosures, supported by detailed 2D technical drawings and a motion animation.
